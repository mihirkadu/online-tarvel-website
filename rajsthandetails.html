Explore Rajasthan — Royal State



A land of forts, deserts, lakes and vibrant cultural heritage. Perfect for history lovers and cultural travellers.




Top Cities & Highlights

  • Jaipur — Palaces & bazaars
  • Udaipur — Lake City & City Palace
  • Jodhpur — Mehrangarh Fort
  • Jaisalmer — Golden Fort & Sam Sand Dunes
  • Pushkar — Holy lake & annual camel fair

Suggested 7-Day Itinerary

  1. Day 1-2: Jaipur (Amer Fort, City Palace, markets)
  2. Day 3: Pushkar (Lake, Brahma Temple)
  3. Day 4-5: Jodhpur (Mehrangarh, local markets)
  4. Day 6: Jaisalmer + Evening Desert Camp
  5. Day 7: Return / Optional Udaipur detour

Activities

Camel safaris in Jaisalmer, folk performances, heritage walks, village tours and craft workshops (block printing, blue pottery).

Travel Tips & Essentials

  • Carry cash for smaller shops/markets.
  • Respect local customs—dress modestly in rural areas.
  • Plan desert stays with licensed tour operators for safety.
